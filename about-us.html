About Us

Who We are ?

Welcome to Coochbehar Polyclinic!

Over the years we have grown to offer a comprehensive range of top-quality healthcare services across the region and we are popular for our quality care and emergency services. Being one of the leading healthcare practice center in Cooch Behar, we offer our patients several alternative highly-effective methods of treatment specifically designed to meet their individual needs.

Our Core Values

We care for your health as no one does!

Our works are guided by key values. We believe that patients deserve to have timely access to healthcare and that our system should reflect this value.

Excellence: We support the best standard of care, personal and professional growth.

Dignity: For us, patients come first. We honour and respect every person we encounter as a valued part of the human family.

Well being: We promote the full integration of our patient’s physical and mental health in order to enable them to participate in their own health improvement

Social justice: We collaborate with others to improve the services we provide to do justice to our patient's faith and belief that they have kept in us.
